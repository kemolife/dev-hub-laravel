In late 2024 we made a decision that felt obvious at the time: we'd consolidate our infrastructure by moving our queue workload from Redis onto Postgres. One database, fewer services, simpler ops.

The team agreed in twenty minutes.

What followed was three months of progressively worse production incidents, a rewrite of our worker layer, and a careful walk-back to a setup that, in hindsight, almost any senior engineer should have predicted.
